Cattle Health Monitoring System

This is a monitoring system that uses Rule based and Machine learning analysis on cattle information for quick diagnosis of diseases affecting your cattle.

Vital signs analysis. This tool helps in analysing your cattle vital signs history to help you identify anomalies using rule based analysis.
